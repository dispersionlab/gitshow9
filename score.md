### module2 description
module2.js is logical comparator. It takes signal inputs in Inputs 1 & 2, and outputs the Max, Min, Mix of the two signals out Outputs 1, 2, 3, respectively. 

- Inputs: IN1, IN2
- Outputs: OUT1, OUT2, OUT3
